Ganesh Chaturti Activity Involves!

The Ganesh Chaturthi celebration features a variety of engaging activities. It begins with the installation of Ganesh idols in the college premises, accompanied by traditional prayers and rituals. Cultural performances, including music, dance, and drama, honor Lord Ganesha and entertain the audience. The college campus is adorned with colorful decorations, rangolis, and floral arrangements, creating a festive atmosphere. Prasad distribution symbolizes blessings and abundance, fostering a sense of communal sharing. Additionally, workshops, lectures, and discussions are organized to educate students about the significance of Ganesh Chaturthi and its customs. Community service activities, such as environmental conservation initiatives and charity drives, reflect the spirit of giving associated with the festival. Overall, the celebration of Ganesh Chaturthi in the college encompasses devotion, cultural enrichment, and community engagement
