Purpose!

  • Celebrate and honor the birth of Lord Krishna
  • Educate students about Janmashtami traditions
  • Promote unity and harmony within the school community

Outcome!

  • Increased cultural awareness and appreciation among students
  • Strengthened sense of community and unity among participants

Janmashtami Activity Involves!

The Janmashtami celebration includes a variety of engaging activities. Traditional puja ceremonies mark the beginning, followed by vibrant cultural performances such as devotional songs, dance presentations, and skits portraying Krishna's life. The school premises are adorned with colorful decorations and thematic displays. Prasad distribution symbolizes blessings, while workshops and storytelling sessions educate about Janmashtami's significance. Additionally, charity drives and community service activities underscore Krishna's message of compassion and service to others.
