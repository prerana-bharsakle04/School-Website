Mid-day meals conducted in our schools typically involve several key components to ensure that students receive nutritious and balanced meals. It involves careful consideration of nutritional guidelines and local dietary preferences to create balanced and culturally appropriate meals. Menu includes a combination of staple foods such as rice, wheat, or millets, along with protein-rich items like lentils, beans, or eggs, and seasonal fruits and vegetables.
